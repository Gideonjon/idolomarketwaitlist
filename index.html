What is Idolo Mart?

I am an online marketplace for buying and selling used tech gadegets such as smartphones, smartwatches, laptops, tablets,cameras, drones,gaming console and more.

How does Idolomart work?

I connect buyers and sellers directly, without any middlemen or intermediaries. sellers create listings of their devices, upload photos and descriptions, go through our rigorous testing and set their own prices. Buyers browse the listings, ask questions and make offers. once agreed upon, the buyer pays for the item via our multiple modes of payment, we engage escrow which provides protection for both parties. The seller carefully packs and then ships the device with our partner logistics provider to the buyer, and the buyer buyer confirms receipt and condition of the device. Idolomart staffs are always available to help with any issues or disputes that might arise during transactions.
